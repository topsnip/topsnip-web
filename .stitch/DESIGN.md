## Creative North Star: The Digital Curator

This design system rejects the "boxed-in" nature of traditional web interfaces. It is built on the philosophy of **The Digital Curator** — a high-end, editorial approach where every pixel serves as a frame for content.

By prioritizing generous whitespace (the "Luxury of Space") and utilizing tonal depth instead of structural lines, we create an experience that feels organic, premium, and authoritative.

| Outline | `#717785` | Borders, dividers |
| Outline Variant | `#c1c6d6` | Ghost borders (15% opacity) |

### Rules
- **No-Line Rule**: Do not use 1px solid borders. Use background color shifts.
- **Glassmorphism**: Use `#ffffff` at 80% opacity with `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`.
- **Gradients**: CTAs use `#0071e3` → `#0059b5` linear gradient.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Pill-shaped, `#0071e3` fill, white text
- **Secondary**: Pill-shaped, `#717785` border (20% opacity), `#0059b5` text
- **Tertiary**: Text-only, `#0059b5`, underline on hover

### Cards
- 12px corner radius
- `#ffffff` background with 4% ambient shadow
- No divider lines — use spacing instead

### Navigation (Glass Bar)
- Sticky, glassmorphism (80% white + blur)
- Height: 3.5rem
- Label-medium for nav links
